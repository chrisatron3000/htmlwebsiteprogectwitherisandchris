Who are tornado hunters and what do they do?

May 13, 2026 Eris Shramek

Tornado Hunters are people who actively seek out and chase tornadoes. Most people who do this are well-trained on how to stay safe and are collecting data for a weather service or to study how tornadoes form. Although, some people chase tornadoes for fun or for a blog.

Having people who hunt tornadoes and record data has been extremly imporant to the development of what we know about tornadoes and our overall understanding of them.
